Chicken Stir-fry and Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts: 175g
  • Spices: Salt, Pepper, Minced garlic, dried or fresh ginger, and chili paste.
  • Bell Pepper: 1
  • Large Onion: 1
  • Broccoli: 1lb
  • Carrots: 1
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cooked Rice
  1. Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized cubes.
  2. Add the spices to the chicken and mix well.
  3. Chop up the bell pepper, large onion, broccoli, and carrots.
  4. Put them on a pan and add a dash of soy sauce.
  5. Cook the vegetables on medium heat and add the chicken back in.
  6. Fry the contents until the chicken is golden brown.
  7. Serve the dish with some cooked rice, and Enjoy!
