Chicken Burrito

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast: 150g
  • Spices: Paprika, Minced Garlic, and Black Pepper
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Sweet Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Lemon Juice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Whole Wheat Tortilla
  • Onion
  1. Cut the Chicken into small bite-sized chunks.
  2. Add paprika, garlic, lemon juice, and black pepper to the chicken and mix well.
  3. On a stove, cook the chicken until golden brown.
  4. To make the sauce, add yogurt, sweet pepper, parsley, and lemon juice to a blender and blend it.
  5. Chop up some lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  6. Add them to a whole wheat tortilla along with the chicken and the sauce.
  7. Wrap up the torilla and Enjoy!
