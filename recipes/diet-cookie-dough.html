High Protein Cookie Dough

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Sugar Free Maple Syrup
  • Whey Protein powder
  • Chocolate Chips
  1. In a bowl, add a spoonfull a peanut butter, maple sugar, a scoop of whey protein powder.
  2. Mix the contents well and then add the desired amount of Chocolate chips to the mix.
  3. Continue to mix throughly, refridgerate the cookie dough for an hour, and Enjoy!
